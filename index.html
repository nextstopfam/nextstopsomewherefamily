Quick Travel Tips

  • Pack in categories: snacks, comfort, “oops kit” (wipes, bags, meds).
  • Save locations offline: map pins + addresses before you leave.
  • Two-outfit rule: one comfy travel outfit + one backup.
  • Kid win: small surprise snack for the halfway point.
  • Photo tip: remove GPS metadata before uploading.
